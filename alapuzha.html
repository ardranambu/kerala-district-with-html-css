ALAPUZHA

**Alappuzha**, often called the “Venice of the East,” is famous for its enchanting network of canals, lagoons, and backwaters. It is renowned for the traditional **houseboat cruises** that offer visitors a unique way to explore Kerala’s scenic backwaters and lush landscapes. Alappuzha is also a major center for coir manufacturing and seafood processing. The annual **Alappuzha Beach Festival** and the famous **Neermahal Snake Boat Race** during the Onam season showcase the region’s vibrant culture and traditions. With its charming waterways, historic temples, and serene beaches, Alappuzha is a must-visit destination for experiencing Kerala’s natural beauty and cultural richness.
