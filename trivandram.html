THIRUVANANTHAPURAM

Trivandrum, the capital city of Kerala, is renowned for its blend of tradition and modernity. It is famous for the iconic **Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple**, one of the richest and most architecturally stunning temples in India. The city is also a major center for education and research, housing prestigious institutions like the **Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER)** and **Technopark**, India’s largest IT park. With its beautiful beaches like **Kovalam**, lush green surroundings, and rich cultural heritage including classical dance and festivals, Trivandrum offers a unique mix of spirituality, nature, and progress. The city is also a gateway to Kerala’s famous backwaters and hill stations.
